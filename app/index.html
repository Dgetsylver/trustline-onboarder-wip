How it works

Set up an asset in about a minute.

No jargon, no waiting room. You stay in control the whole time — Authline only ever asks for a single signature.

The usual way

1 “Create a trustline?” — for what?
2 Sign, and hope it’s right
3 Wait for the issuer to approve you
4 Maybe your withdrawal goes through

With Authline

1 Tap “Activate”
2 Confirm once in your wallet
3 You’re ready to receive — instantly
1

Connect

Open the app and connect any Stellar wallet — Freighter, xBull, Lobstr and more.

2

Confirm once

A single signature adds the asset and gets it approved in the same step. You just cover the tiny network fee.

3

You’re in

The asset is live on your account. Withdrawals clear and you can receive right away.

For issuers & exchanges

Set the rules once. Approvals run themselves.

Instead of a person signing off on every new holder, you set your policy up front — and Authline enforces it automatically, in the open, on a ledger anyone can audit.

That’s what lets regulated issuers bring compliant assets to Stellar without a human in the loop for every withdrawal. List your asset by publishing one standard config block.

Open by default

Stablecoins

Anyone can activate instantly, and you keep the power to block bad actors. Great for open stablecoins that just need frictionless onboarding.

Approved holders only

Securities

Only people you’ve cleared can activate — built for KYC’d or regulated assets where every holder is vetted first.

Authline builds on infrastructure that’s already live on Stellar mainnet — powering EURCV, the euro stablecoin from Société Générale-Forge. It’s the first asset of many.
